About Holland Park West

Your destination for unforgettable celebrations

Where Elegance Meets Celebration

Holland Park West is a unique outdoor venue, where nature meets urban sophistication - to create an extraordinary event for you. It features 3 stunning waterfalls, Rock haven, and Timbers Pavilion which are on oasis of natural beauty. What's more; there is a spacious dining pavilion equipped with a full kitchen that serves only the freshest food to satisfy your guests' appetites.
